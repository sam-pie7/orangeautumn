orangemarketorgans on etsy:

I grew up in a household full of crafters. How could I not follow this path? I love making everything. If I go a day without creating something I am not a happy camper.

A strange obsession with body parts, anatomy, what have you, was strengthened upon taking an anatomy course during my undergrad years. I started drawing organs, and bones, and such, with a bit of my own creative license on these beautiful structures. I wanted to learn more about every crevice and corner of our bodies. And I absolutely loved vintage anatomical drawings, think Gray's Anatomy and the like. enjoy!
